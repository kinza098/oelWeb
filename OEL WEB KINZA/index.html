BRIDAL SHOWER EVENT

A bridal shower is a party specifically for the bride. No men allowed. The main purpose of the party is for all your lady friends to come over (or go out to a restaurant or some other destination), and shower you with gifts in anticipation for your new life as a married woman.

BIRTHDAY PARTY

You'll help with the development, planning, and execution of events like weddings, birthday parties, and corporate receptions, among others. Your tasks will vary based on the unique needs of each client. But you'll often be tasked with helping them select a venue and then creating a floor plan for the event.

MEHNDI/MAYOON EVENTS

Held a day before the actual wedding, the Mehndi ceremony is a fun-filled, nostalgic sit-down for the bride, her cousins, siblings, friends, aunts and others. This is their day, filled with fun, laughter, songs, dances and games as each sits down for a dollop of Mehndi over their hands and feet.
